## What It Is

Persona LM is an AI buyer intelligence platform for ecommerce. It takes raw purchase history (from Shopify, Amazon, Stripe, CSV, or API) and produces:

1. **Buyer Archetypes** — Distinct buyer types discovered from real purchase behavior, not surveys or demographics. Each archetype gets a name, behavioral profile, conversion rate, price affinity, and category mix.
2. **Demand Forecasting** — Predicts which customers will buy which products. 80% accuracy validated against real held-out transactions.
3. **AI Analyst (Psyche)** — A conversational AI with 26+ tools that answers questions about your customers in plain English, returns structured insight cards with metrics and charts.
4. **Persona Councils** — Pose a product question, select buyer archetypes, see tower-scored votes with evidence and risk levels before you launch.
5. **Ad Intelligence** — Scrapes competitor ads, scores creative against buyer archetypes, generates brand-aware variants, and A/B tests against persona councils before spend.
6. **Reports** — Segment reports, demand forecasts, product-market fit scorecards, exportable as PDF/CSV or shareable links.

## Voice & Tone

### Personality

Persona LM speaks like a sharp, competent analyst who respects the reader's time. Not a marketer. Not a chatbot. An expert colleague who gives you the answer first, then the evidence.

### Voice Attributes

- **Direct** — Lead with the answer, not the setup
- **Precise** — Use numbers, not adjectives. "34% CVR" not "high conversion"
- **Grounded** — Every claim ties back to real data
- **Warm but professional** — Not cold/corporate, not overly casual. The warmth of a trusted advisor.
- **Confident without arrogance** — We know our product works. We don't need to oversell.

### Tone Spectrum

| Dimension                   | Position                                            |
| --------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Formal <-> Casual           | 60% formal — professional but approachable          |
| Technical <-> Simple        | 55% technical — uses domain terms but explains them |
| Serious <-> Playful         | 70% serious — credibility matters, but not sterile  |
| Restrained <-> Enthusiastic | 65% restrained — let the data speak                 |

### Do's

- Use concrete numbers: "80% accuracy", "14,491 buyers", "<60 seconds"
- Use action verbs: "discover", "predict", "score", "ask"
- Frame insights as decisions: "Focus premium lines here" not "This segment has high conversion"
- Reference buyer archetypes by name (Zeus, Athena, Apollo, etc.)
- Use the word "grounded" — it's our differentiator
- Use em dashes for emphasis
- Start with what the user gets, not how it works

### Don'ts

- Don't use "leverage", "utilize", "synergy", "disrupt", "revolutionize"
- Don't say "AI-powered" as a value prop on its own — say what the AI actually does
- Don't use empty superlatives ("best-in-class", "world-class", "cutting-edge")
- Don't lead with features — lead with outcomes
- Don't use jargon without context (ok: "buyer archetypes"; not ok: "learned tower projection head")
- Don't use emojis in marketing copy
- Don't claim certainty on predictions — "80% accuracy" is honest, "guaranteed" is not
- Never sound like a generic SaaS landing page
